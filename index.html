— The Process

Long ferments.
Honest
ingredients.

Viala bakes small. We mix the night before, ferment slow, shape by hand in the morning, and pull loaves out one batch at a time until evening.

What you get is bread that chews the way bread should — open crumb, deep crust, real fermentation flavor. No shortcuts. No mass-bake. Just one neighborhood bakery on Howland, working the way bakers used to.

We're open from 10 AM most days — and we stay open late. Drop by after dinner. There's still good bread on the rack.
